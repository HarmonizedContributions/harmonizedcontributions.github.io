Harmonized Contributions

Harmonized Contributions is a Portugal-based initiative developing digital protocols and applications for cost-of-living coordination, social stabilization, and decentralized contribution frameworks.

Our work focuses on local-first, privacy-preserving systems, including the development of the Pulse protocol, designed to support voluntary participation, reduced administrative overhead, and scalable international pilot programs.

The organization operates at the intersection of technology, public interest infrastructure, and applied research.
